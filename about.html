WHAT WE DO ...

We bring new music to ardent music lovers. Our intimate video portraits invite you directly into the artist’s realm through in-studio or on-location performances and interviews.

We provide songs of the artists you love. Our project grants can be game-changers for artists seeking to record, promote, perform, or otherwise further their work and careers.




HOW WE DO IT ...

We support the musical community. The Melody Muse supports musicians with collaborative opportunities. We bring together songwriters, producers, and performers through workshops, retreats, and showcases to facilitate relationships and encourage artistic cooperation.

We help convene an ecosystem of agents, managers, marketers, producers, lawyers, publicists and others who have a proven track record of competence and enthusiasm for emerging artists to help our musicians avoid some of the pitfalls so common in the music industry.

Music is one of the most powerful tools we have to help people understand, hear, and embrace one another. Supporting the music-making of our emerging artists advances a humane approach to one another and the world around us.
